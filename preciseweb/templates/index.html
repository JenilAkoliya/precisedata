Frequently Asked Questions

  • In what format is the data delivered?

    Choose from JSON, XML, CSV, or Microsoft Excel.

  • Where is the data stored?

    You can select your preferred delivery and storage method: API, Webhook, Amazon S3, Google Cloud, Google Cloud Pubsub, Microsoft Azure, or SFTP.

  • Why is it important to have an unblocking solution when scraping?

    Having an unblocking solution when scraping is important because many websites have anti-scraping measures that block the scraper’s IP address or require CAPTCHA solving. The unblocking solution implemented within Precise Data’s script is designed to bypass these obstacles and continue gathering data without interruption.

  • Is web scraping legal?

    Many people have false impressions about web scraping. That is because it has been widely adopted to retrieve sensitive data regardless of terms of service. Web scraping itself is not illegal as it is just a process for collecting data more easily.

  • Can I scrape data behind a login page?

    Yes, you can scrape data behind a login page easily if you have a functional account on the website.

  • Is the service able to handle large-scale data?

    Yes, as a web scraping service provider company, we are equipped to handle large-scale data. We have the infrastructure, resources, and expertise to efficiently extract and process vast amounts of data from websites. Our web scraping solutions are designed to handle diverse data requirements, whether it involves scraping thousands of web pages, crawling through extensive websites, or extracting information from massive datasets. We employ scalable and distributed systems to handle large volumes of data effectively. Our team of experts can develop customized scraping strategies, implement robust data extraction algorithms, and ensure the efficient storage and management of the scraped data. Additionally, we can provide data cleansing, normalization, and formatting services to ensure that the extracted data is accurate, reliable, and ready for analysis or integration into your systems. Our aim is to deliver high-quality and comprehensive datasets, tailored to meet your specific requirements, even when dealing with large-scale data.
